About AstroLifeGPS

An AI-powered life navigation system for the decisions that matter.

AstroLifeGPS is building an AI-powered life navigation system — a daily, personalised guide that helps people decide when to act on the things that matter: their career, money, relationships, and health.

Why we exist

Most people use GPS to navigate roads. We think the same idea — real-time, personalised, always-on guidance — belongs to life's bigger decisions too. Traditional astrology apps stop at a generic daily horoscope. AstroLifeGPS goes further: it reads your unique birth chart and live planetary conditions and turns them into a clear, daily roadmap.

How it works

At the core is ReBi AI™, our intelligence layer purpose-built for Vedic computation. It combines natal mathematics, live transit overlays, dasha logic, and conversational reasoning into a single pipeline — producing your daily favourability scores, Golden Moments, and an AI astrologer you can simply talk to.

Where we are

We are a focused team based in Bengaluru, India, working at the intersection of ancient timing systems and modern artificial intelligence. AstroLifeGPS is operated by AstroLifeGPS Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
