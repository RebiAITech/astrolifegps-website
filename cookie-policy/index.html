Last updated: June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how AstroLifeGPS uses cookies and similar technologies on our website at astrolifegps.com, and equivalent device technologies in our mobile app. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They help the site function, remember your preferences, and understand how it is used. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, software development kits (SDKs) in mobile apps, and pixel tags — this policy covers all of them.

3. Third-Party Technologies

Some features rely on third-party services (such as payment providers or app-store SDKs) that may set their own cookies or identifiers. We do not control these and recommend reviewing the privacy policies of those services.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings — viewing stored cookies, deleting them, and blocking some or all of them (note that blocking essential cookies may break parts of the website). On mobile, you can manage tracking and reset advertising identifiers through your device's privacy settings.
